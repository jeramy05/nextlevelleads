About Us

Next Level Leads was launched utilizing public posting pages to gather prospect information as they list their vehicles. 

Our entire process is built around trade-ins. Prospects list their vehicles for sale we our software goes through each listing pulls contact information and is then assigned to our representatives.

Once we determine prospect intentions we distribute leads as specified by dealer and sales representative partners.

We can focus in on specific vehicles makes and prospects within a 20 mile radius of your location.
